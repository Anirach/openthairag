Title: อ่าวใบลาน (Bai Lan Bay)
Content: อ่าวใบลานมีลักษณะเป็นหาดทรายยาวเงียบสงบเหมาะแก่การเล่นน้ำและพักผ่อน Ao Bai Lan (Bai Lan bay) has characteristic sandy beaches alternating with rocky bays. It is one of the more popular destinations among foreigners. Accommodation, in a price range to suit all, is found at regular intervals from the start of the beach as far south as Ao Bang Bao; prices are similar to those at Haad Sai Kaow and range from budget (200 500 Baht) up to 3,500 Baht . sidewalk restaurants and coffee shops line the street.
